CoderDojo Dublin @ The Warehouse

Who are you?

CoderDojo Dublin @ The Warehouse is a programming club for young people. At the Dojo, young people, aged 6 thru 17, learn how to code, develop websites, apps, programs, games, and explore technology in an informal and creative environment.

We typically run sessions covering the following topics:

  • Scratch for novices to learn the basics of programming
  • HTML/CSS to create your own web sites
  • JavaScript to bring your web pages to life
  • Python for anybody who has already mastered Scratch
  • App inventor to create applications for Android phones and tablets
  • Projects for experienced programmers working on their own hardware and/or software projects

Where and when do you meet?

We meet every Saturday, except Bank Holiday weekends, during the school term from 1pm to 4pm at the offices of Zalando at The Warehouse, 35 Barrow Street, Dublin 4.

Getting there by bus?

Barrow Street is served by the 56a and 77a Dublin Bus routes.

Getting there by train?

Get off the at Grand Canal Dock DART station, turn left upon exiting the station and walk for about 2 minutes.

Coming by car?

There is limited parking available at Zalando's offices on a first-come first-served basis. Please do not use the spaces in-front of building.

Do I need a ticket?

Yes, but they're free!

Tickets are avaible on EventBrite from 7pm on the Monday before the session until midnight on the Friday.

What should I bring?

  • A laptop - We don't have any spare laptops so you'll need to bring your own or borrow one
  • A parent - If you are under 13, a parent must stay with you throughout the session
  • A drink and a snack - There aren't many places nearby to eat
